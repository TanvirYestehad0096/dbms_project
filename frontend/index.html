Frequently Asked Questions

General Information

The Bangladesh Citizen Card System is an official government portal that allows citizens to apply for, manage, and download their National Identity Cards.

Regarding Card Correction

If your NID card contains incorrect information, you can submit a correction request through this portal with the required supporting documents.

About Lost Card

If your NID card has been lost or stolen, you can apply for a re-issue through this portal. A GD from a local police station may be required.

How to Register

Click "Get Started", provide your NID number, date of birth, and phone number for OTP verification, then follow the on-screen steps.

Bangladesh Digital Card System

The Bangladesh Digital Card System provides citizens with a centralized platform to access and manage multiple government and service-related digital cards securely.

Citizens can register accounts, apply for new cards, update existing information, monitor application status, and access downloadable digital card copies through a modern online system.
